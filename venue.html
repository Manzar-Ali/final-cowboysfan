Venues

The Cowboys FanZ Club.. Established 2006, Now Entering our 16th Season!

North Texas:
We are back for our 5th season at II Brothers Sports Grill & Bar in Plano Texas. This Venue has 65 and 75 inch televisions at every angle. A Family friendly venue with an amazing menu and friendly staff.

Southern California:
The Society Of Advanced Home Wine Makings Event Venue in Ontario California is once again our venue for the 2021/2022 NFL Season. Our 2nd year here, and we look forward to more great times! The menu changes every game, and the pricing on food and drinks is the best in the area. Come check out this gem of a venue and enjoy a Cowboys Game with us!

Be sure to tell your family and friends to come join us at II Brothers, where the Cowboys FanZ meet to party in North Dallas with a fun & family friendly atmosphere for EVERY GAME!


If you own, or know of a venue which would like to host the Cowboys FanZ Club please have them contact us about bringing the clubs game watch events to your area. Have them reach out to us at: don@dallascowboysfanz.com
